Sustainability Initiative

Turn Waste into Rewards

Our unique recycling program lets users exchange plastic bottles and sorted food waste for KIKAPU credits that can be used to purchase fresh, local produce.

How It Works:

  • Collect plastic bottles and separate your food waste
  • Drop off at any KIKAPU collection point
  • Earn credits based on weight and quality
  • Use credits to purchase fresh produce and products

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about careers at KIKAPU.

Do you offer internship opportunities?

Yes! We offer both paid internships and apprenticeships across various departments. These programs are designed to provide meaningful experience while contributing to our mission. Internship opportunities are posted on our careers page seasonally.

Is KIKAPU only hiring in specific locations?

While some roles (especially those in operations and vendor relations) require a physical presence in our operating markets, many positions are remote-friendly. Each job posting specifies the location requirements.

What kind of background is KIKAPU looking for?

We value diverse backgrounds and perspectives. While specific roles may require certain technical skills or experience, we're generally looking for individuals who are passionate about sustainability, local food systems, and community building.

How can I stay updated on new job openings?

You can sign up for job alerts on our careers page to receive notifications when new positions matching your interests are posted. You can also follow us on social media where we announce new opportunities.

Application Process

How to Join Our Team

Our hiring process is designed to be transparent, fair, and focused on finding the right match for both you and KIKAPU.

1

Apply Online

Submit your application through our careers portal. Be sure to include a cover letter explaining why you're passionate about KIKAPU's mission.

2

Initial Interview

If your application is selected, you'll have a video call with our hiring team to discuss your experience and interest in the role.

3

Technical Assessment

Depending on the role, you may be asked to complete a skill-based assessment or project to demonstrate your capabilities.

4

Team Interview

Meet with potential team members and peers to get a better understanding of the day-to-day responsibilities and team dynamics.

5

Final Interview

A final discussion with department leadership to ensure alignment with KIKAPU's mission, values, and expectations.

6

Offer & Onboarding

If selected, you'll receive an offer and begin our comprehensive onboarding program to set you up for success.
