Refund Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2025

Overview

At Kikapu Marketplace, we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with every purchase. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds and returns for products purchased through our marketplace.

Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund in the following circumstances:

  • The product received is damaged, defective, or doesn't match the description
  • The delivery is significantly delayed beyond the promised delivery window
  • The product is missing essential components or items ordered
  • The quality of fresh produce doesn't meet our quality standards

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Kikapu account
  2. Navigate to "Orders" in your account dashboard
  3. Select the specific order
  4. Click on "Request Refund" and follow the instructions
  5. Submit clear photos of the product if applicable

Alternatively, you can contact our customer support team at support@kikapu.co.tz or call +255 (0) 123 456 789 within 24 hours of receiving your order.

Refund Process

Once your refund request is received, our team will:

  1. Review your request within 24-48 hours
  2. Communicate with the vendor or supplier if necessary
  3. Process approved refunds within 3-5 business days
  4. Notify you once the refund has been processed

Refunds will be issued using the original payment method. For mobile money payments, refunds will be sent to the same phone number used for the original transaction.

Special Considerations for Fresh Produce

For fresh fruits, vegetables, and other perishable items:

  • Report quality issues immediately upon delivery
  • Refund requests must be made within 24 hours of delivery
  • Photo evidence of the product condition is required

We understand the unique challenges of delivering fresh local produce and are committed to ensuring you receive only the highest quality items from our partner farmers and vendors.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items or situations may not be eligible for refunds:

  • Items damaged due to customer mishandling after delivery
  • Custom-prepared food items (for food safety reasons)
  • Products with broken seals, unless they arrived damaged
  • Cases where delivery was attempted but customer wasn't available

Store Credit Option

In some cases, we may offer store credit as an alternative to a monetary refund. Store credit will be added to your Kikapu account and can be used for future purchases.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: support@kikapu.co.tz
  • By phone: +255 (0) 123 456 789
  • By mail: Kikapu Marketplace, Plot 123, Njiro Road, Arusha, Tanzania
