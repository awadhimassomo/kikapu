How to Earn Kikapu Credits

Earn While Shopping

Every time you shop on the Kikapu platform, you'll earn credits worth 5% of your purchase value.

Purchase from local vendors and earn credits automatically

Buy fresh produce and earn while supporting local farmers

Use these credits for future purchases or other benefits

Example:

If you spend TSh 20,000 on groceries, you'll earn TSh 1,000 in Kikapu Credits!

Earn Through Recycling

Help the environment and earn credits by collecting and sorting recyclable waste. Earn TSh 200 per kilogram of sorted trash!

Collect recyclable materials like plastic, paper, and metals

Sort the trash by material type for maximum credit value

Bring your collected waste to our designated recycling points

Example:

If you collect and sort 5kg of recyclable waste, you'll earn TSh 1,000 in Kikapu Credits!
