Frequently Asked Questions

Who should schedule a pitch meeting?

If you're an investor, technology partner, or an organization looking to explore strategic collaboration with Kikapu, scheduling a pitch meeting is a perfect starting point.

How should I prepare for the meeting?

Come prepared with your key questions and objectives. For investor pitches, consider what information you need beyond what's in our investor pack. For partnerships, outline potential synergies you see with our platform.

What happens after I submit this form?

Our team will review your request and reach out within 48 hours to confirm a meeting time or request additional information. We'll provide you with a calendar invite and meeting link if it's a virtual meeting.
