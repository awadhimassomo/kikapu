Frequently Asked Questions

Prepaid cards require you to load money before you can spend it. You can only spend what you have already loaded.

Postpaid cards allow you to buy things now and pay for them later, up to your credit limit. These cards require a good spending history with Kikapu.

To qualify for a Postpaid Kikapu Card, you need to:

  • Have spent at least TSh 100,000 on groceries via Kikapu
  • Have a good credit score with us (minimum 500 points)
  • Pay the annual maintenance fee of TSh 2,000

If you don't qualify yet, you can start with a Prepaid card and build your history with us.

Both Prepaid and Postpaid card holders can receive a 20% discount during holiday seasons if:

  • They have a good credit score (650+)
  • Their card is active and in good standing
  • They have made regular purchases on the platform

Discounts are automatically applied during checkout when a holiday campaign is running.
